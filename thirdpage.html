Wealth Distribution

The main exports of Japan are manufactured products, machinery, and automobiles. China (22.0% of Japan's total exports in 2020), the United States (18.5%), and the Republic of Korea (7.3%) were the top three countries for Japanese goods exports (7.0 per cent). Imports Crude oil ($38.4 billion), petroleum gas ($31.4 billion), integrated circuits ($17.7 billion), broadcasting equipment ($17.5 billion), and computers ($14.5 billion) are Japan's top five imports.

"In 2020, the proportion of adults in Japan with wealth between $100,000 and $1,000,000 was around 53%; the second-largest group had wealth between $10,000 and $100,000. These groups made up about 86 percent of the population as a whole. In Japan, levels of relative poverty and income inequality among people of working age have exceeded the OECD average. "
