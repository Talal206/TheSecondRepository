Industry

Tokyo has some really bold aspirations for growth. According to a recent assessment by the Tokyo Metropolitan government (TMG), Japan's capital will grow to be bigger, better, and include a small metropolis. As its name implies, Tokyo eSG will be designed on the fundamentals of social and environmental governance and combine cutting-edge green technologies. By the year 2050, "the world's first ESG city" is expected to be finished and serve as a role model for the entire world.

Other crops produced in Japan include a wide range of fruits and vegetables, wheat, soybeans, and barley. Automobiles, consumer electronics, computers, semiconductors, copper, and iron and steel are among Japan's top export products.
